Bayard Acres Labrador Retrievers.

BAYARD ACRES LABRADOR RETRIEVERS. As a breeder, we aim to follow strict standards for maintaining genetically defect free Labrador Retrievers. All of our dogs have been tested and cleared for EIC OAC eyesOur puppies are whelped and raised in our home and are acclimated to our family environment where our children are a part of the process. The puppies will grow and learn to be comfortable with young children by the time they are ready to leave their home. At Bayard Acres Labrador Retrievers, we believe that our dogs are very much a part of the family and should be given the attention that they deserve. They are a companion for life and are a commitment that should not be taken lightly. If you are considering bringing a Labrador retriever into your home, you need to be sure that you can give it the attention that it deserves and that you are committed to carring for your pet for the long haul.
