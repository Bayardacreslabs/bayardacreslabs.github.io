BAYARD ACRES LABRADOR RETRIEVERS. How do I purchase a puppy from Bayard Acres Labrador Retrievers? You must make a non-refundable deposit of $250 to ensure that you have secured a puppy from our litters. We except Paypal or a cashier\92s check. Paypal is the best way to ensure that you secure your place in the selection process. What is the selection process for my puppy? When you make your deposit you will select a male or a female. Once we have received your deposit with indication of gender for your puppy, we will place you in the order for selection based on when we have received the deposit. On the day that we determine the puppies are ready to pick up, you will need to come and pick up your puppy in the order of your pick and at the time that we designate. What Kind of Guarantee does Bayard Acres Labs Offer? We offer a 1 year guarantee on genetic defects related to hip displaysia, exercise induce collapse and that they are free of genetically inherited eye disease. Your puppy will be healthy when you pick it up and it will be dewormed and have its 6 week vaccinations.When Can We See The Puppies? You can come and see the puppies at 7 weeks old after they have received their vaccinations. This is also the time when you can choose your puppy, purchase it and take it home. We will not do business on Sunday as we honor the Lords day and set it apart.We will post pictures of the puppies on our website after 4 weeks of age. If you want more pictures of the puppies, I can provide them after you have made a deposit on a puppy.
